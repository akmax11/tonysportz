Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Pixel Playground ("we") believes privacy is a game everyone wins. Our website does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not collect personal data unless you voluntarily send a message via the contact form (which is currently a demo and does not store data). Future versions may use anonymous local analytics, but we will never sell or share your info. All games run entirely in your browser – no data leaves your device.

Local storage: The clicker game saves your total clicks in your browser's local storage (only if you agree via the prompt). You can clear it anytime. Our memory game and tic‑tac‑toe don't store anything.

If you have questions, email privacy@pixelplay.fun (fake domain for demo).
